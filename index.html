FORWARD BENDING

Instructions

Sit down with your legs stretched out in front of you. Exhale, and bend over towards your knees as far as you can go. Try to hold the position for 1 minute.

Breathe calmly, and use the contraction of the lungs on the exhales to stretch even lower.

What you'll learn

Follow he Wim Holf Method as it is practiced by the Iceman himself. Every time Wim changes up his routine, you get a new set of daily challenges. One for the morning, one for the afternoon, and one for the evening. Use progress bars and daily reminders to help you stay on track. Each new routine has a physical component, such as working on your push-ups or improving your headstand; and a general health goal, like cutting out sugars for a month. The purpose of these challenges is to improve your capacity for commitment, which you can then utilize across all arenas of life. As a bonus, you get a nice toned physique.

Before your session

Please sit or lie down, in a safe environment.

WHM breathing can affect motor control and, in rare cases, lead to loss of consciousness. Always sit or lie down before practicing the techniques. Never practice while piloting a vehicle, or in or near bodies of water.

The Wim Hof Method is about learning to find comfort in discomfort, rather than setting records of fighting through pain. Listen to your body, not your ego.
